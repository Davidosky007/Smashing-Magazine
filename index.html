PERSON OF THE WEEK

Manuel Matuzović (@mmatuzo) is a front-end developer and consultant with a passion for accessibility, progressive enhancement, performance, and web standards. He works for the City of Vienna, where he’s responsible for the front end of wien.gv.at. Manuel loves learning new things and shares his knowledge as an author, speaker, and teacher. To bring together the local web community in Vienna, he co-organizes the Webclerks meetup. Thank you for sharing and caring, dear Manuel!
